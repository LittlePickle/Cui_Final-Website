Cemetery of Ashes

After the bell rings, the protagonist climbs out of the sarcophagus in the graveyard and enters a fantasy adventure journey. Pay attention to investigate the red traces on the ground, you will get a prompt message from the system to prompt you on the operation. Here you have to learn to move, switch perspective, combat action and other basic operations.
